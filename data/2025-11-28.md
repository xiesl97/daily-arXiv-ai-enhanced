### [1] [Axion Constraints from White Dwarf Cooling in 47 Tucanae](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.21676)
*Leesa Fleury,Alysa Obertas,Harvey Richer,Jeremy Heyl*

Main category: astro-ph.SR

TL;DR: 研究通过分析47 Tucanae球状星团中白矮星的冷却过程，寻找轴子发射的证据。模拟结果表明，最优模型不支持轴子发射，并给出了轴子-电子耦合常数的更严格上限。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探讨轴子是否存在及其与电子的耦合是否影响白矮星的冷却速率，基于银河系白矮星的异常冷却现象提出假设。

Method: 进行恒星演化模拟，考虑轴子辐射导致的额外能量损失，生成不同参数下的白矮星冷却模型，并通过Hubble Space Telescope观测数据进行比较分析。

Result: 最优模型不支持轴子发射，给出了更严格的轴子-电子耦合上限（$g_{aee} \leq 0.81 \times 10^{-13}$），排除了此前银河系白矮星异常冷却现象提出的轴子参数范围。

Conclusion: 研究结果表明47 Tucanae白矮星的冷却行为不支持轴子发射的存在，并提供了更严格的耦合常数限制。

Abstract: We analyse the cooling of white dwarfs in the globular cluster 47 Tucanae to look for evidence of axion emission affecting the rate of white dwarf cooling. If axions exist and couple to electrons, then axions could be produced at an appreciable rate in the electron-degenerate core of a white dwarf through axion bremsstrahlung from electrons. The emission of these axions would provide an additional cooling mechanism for white dwarfs that would affect the cooling rate, and hints of axions have been suggested based on observations of anomalous cooling reported for white dwarfs in the Galactic disc and halo. We performed stellar evolution simulations of white dwarf cooling that accounted for the additional energy loss due to axion bremsstrahlung from electrons, producing a suite of white dwarf cooling models for different values of the axion-electron coupling constant, as well as the white dwarf mass and envelope thickness. These cooling models are compared to observations of white dwarfs in 47 Tucanae from the Hubble Space Telescope through an unbinned likelihood analysis. The optimal model found by this analysis corresponds to the case of no axion emission with a thick white dwarf envelope, and we find a new bound on the axion-electron coupling of $g_{aee} \leq 0.81 \times 10^{-13}$ at 95% confidence level. This improves upon the previous white dwarf cooling bound for this coupling and excludes the range of values favoured by the axion hints from the anomalous cooling of Galactic white dwarfs.

### [2] [Transit Timing of the White Dwarf-Cold Jupiter System WD 1856+534](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.21611)
*Eli A. Gendreau-Distler,Kate B. Bostow,Kishore C. Patra,Efrain Alvarado,Andreas Betz,Victoria M. Brendel,Vidhi Chander,Asia A. DeGraw,Cooper Jacobus,Connor F. Jennings,Ann Mina,Ansel Parke,Riley Patlak,Neil R. Pichay,Sophia Risin,Edgar P. Vidal,William Wu,Thomas G. Brink,WeiKang Zheng,Alexei V. Filippenko*

Main category: astro-ph.EP

TL;DR: 论文研究了白矮星-冷木星系WD 1856+534的周期性变暗现象（transit timing），发现行星不太可能在当前位置存活于恒星的红巨星阶段，并通过新的观测数据排除了轨道增长的证据。研究还限制了行星的潮汐质量因子和系统中是否存在伴星的可能性，并指出当前轨道的低偏心率表明迁移过程已停止。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索WD 1856+534系统中行星如何到达当前的短半长轴轨道，以及可能的迁移机制和潮汐效应。

Method: 扩展了观测数据的基线（从311个时间点到1498个），分析了新的周期性变暗测量数据，评估了轨道周期导数、行星潮汐质量因子和伴星的可能性。

Result: 数据支持恒定周期模型，行星潮汐质量因子下限为Q_p'≥3.1×10^6，排除了质量大于4.1M_J、周期短于1500天的伴星存在，且当前轨道的偏心率上限为e≲10^-2。

Conclusion: 如果行星是通过高偏心潮汐迁移（HETM）到达当前轨道，则当前的低偏心率表明迁移过程已停止，未来的轨道演化将仅由弱行星潮汐主导。

Abstract: We present new transit timing measurements for the white dwarf-cold Jupiter system WD 1856+534, extending the baseline of observations from 311 epochs to 1498 epochs. The planet is unlikely to have survived the host star's red-giant phase at its present location and is likely too small for common-envelope evolution to take place. As such, a plausible explanation for the short semimajor axis is that the exoplanet started out on a much larger orbit and then spiraled inward through high-eccentricity tidal migration (HETM). A past transit-timing analysis found tentative evidence for orbital growth, which could have been interpreted as a residual effect of HETM, but we find the data are consistent with a constant-period model after adding 18 new transit measurements. We use the estimated period derivative $\dot{P} = 0.04\pm0.43$ ms yr$^{-1}$ to place a lower limit on the planetary tidal quality factor of $Q_p' \gtrsim 3.1 \times 10^6$, consistent with that of Jupiter in our own Solar System. We also test for the presence of companion planets in the system, which could have excited WD 1856 b onto an eccentric orbit via the Kozai-Lidov process, and ultimately rule out the presence of an additional planet with a mass greater than $4.1\,M_J$ and a period shorter than 1500 days. We find no evidence for nonzero eccentricity, with an upper limit of $e\lesssim10^{-2}$. If the planet indeed reached its current orbit through HETM, the low present-day eccentricity indicates that the migration process has now ceased, and any further orbital evolution will be governed solely by weak planetary tides.

### [3] [Evidence For A Correlation Between Astrophysical Neutrinos and Radio Flares](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.21627)
*Yjan A. Gordon,Peter S. Ferguson,Eric J. Hooper,Michael N. Martinez*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 分析了VLASS和IceCube数据，发现射电耀斑与中微子之间存在关联，射电耀斑可能贡献约13%的天体中微子，未来数据有望强化这一发现。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索射电变源与天体物理中微子之间的关联，以识别中微子的电磁对应体。

Method: 利用VLASS射电巡天和IceCube中微子观测站的前两个时期数据，搜索射电变源与中微子的相关性。

Result: 发现射电耀斑与中微子在统计显著性>2σ时存在关联，且80%以上的射电耀斑未在γ射线或X射线波段检测到。未来数据可能将显著性提高到>5σ。

Conclusion: 射电观测在识别中微子的电磁对应体中具有重要作用，未来数据有望验证并强化这一发现。

Abstract: We use data from the first two epochs of the Very Large Array Sky Survey (VLASS) and the IceCube Neutrino Observatory to search for evidence of a correlation between radio variability and the detection of astrophysical neutrinos. We find an excess number of associations between flaring radio sources and neutrinos that were detected between the first and second VLASS observations at $>2σ$ confidence. This excess is consistent with radio flares contributing $\sim13\,\%$ of the astrophysical neutrinos observed by IceCube. Notably $>80\,\%$ of the radio flares associated with neutrinos are not detected at either $γ$-ray or X-ray wavelengths, highlighting the importance of radio observations for identifying potential electromagnetic counterparts to astrophysical neutrinos. No excess in the number of associations between the wider radio-variable population and the IceCube neutrinos is seen when no time constraint is placed on the neutrino detection. We predict that data from future VLASS epochs will see an excess number of associations between radio flares and neutrinos at the $>3σ$ level, and expected improvements to the positional constraints on the neutrinos may increase that confidence to $>5σ$, should our results be representative.

### [4] [A Super-Eddington, Lensing-Magnified Quasar at $z=5.07$ observed with JWST](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.21618)
*Katherine Panebianco,Minghao Yue,Anna-Christina Eilers,Xiaohui Fan,Feige Wang,Jinyi Yang,Robert A. Simcoe*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: JWST/NIRCam成像证实高红移(z=5.07)类星体J0025-0145未多重成像，未检测到宿主星系；前景透镜星系质量与红移支持单像透镜模型，类星体的高爱丁顿比表明早期宇宙中超大质量黑洞可能存在超爱丁顿增长。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究高红移(z=5.07)类星体J0025-0145的透镜效应及其宿主星系性质，以验证多重成像的可能性并探讨超大质量黑洞在早期宇宙中的生长机制。

Method: 利用JWST/NIRCam的F070W和F480M波段成像分析类星体及其透镜星系，结合HST数据进行SED拟合，估计透镜星系质量和红移，计算类星体的最大放大率和固有爱丁顿比。

Result: 类星体未多重成像且无宿主星系检测；透镜星系质量为log(M∗/M⊙)=11.15±0.16，红移zphot=3.62−0.04+0.06；类星体的最大放大率为μmax=3.2，固有爱丁顿比λEddintrinsic>4.9。

Conclusion: J0025-0145是目前已知z>5类星体中爱丁顿比最高的之一，支持早期宇宙中超大质量黑洞的超爱丁顿增长模型。

Abstract: We present JWST/NIRCam F070W and F480M imaging for a quasar at $z = 5.07$, J0025-0145, which is magnified by a foreground lensing galaxy. Existing Hubble Space Telescope (HST) imaging does not have sufficient spatial resolution to determine whether the background quasar is multiply imaged. Exploiting the sharp PSF of the F070W band, we confirm that the background quasar can be well-described by a single point spread function (PSF), essentially ruling out the existence of multiple lensed images. We do not detect the quasar host galaxy in either the F070W or the F480M band. Using the HST and JWST photometry, we fit the Spectral Energy Distribution (SED) of the foreground galaxy. The estimated mass ($\log(M_{*} / M_{\odot}) = 11.15 \pm 0.16$) and redshift ($z_{\text{phot}} = 3.62_{-0.04}^{+0.06}$) of the foreground galaxy are consistent with a single-image lensing model. We estimate the maximum possible magnification of the quasar to be $μ_{\text{max}} = 3.2$, which implies that the intrinsic Eddington ratio of the quasar is at least $λ_{\text{Edd}}^{\text{intrinsic}} > 4.9$. Therefore, J0025-0145 has one of the highest Eddington ratios among $z>5$ supermassive black holes known so far, suggesting the viability of super-Eddington growth for supermassive black holes in the early universe.

</details>
