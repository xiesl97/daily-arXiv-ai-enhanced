### [1] [Dynamical evolution of stellar binaries in galactic centers](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.02905)
*Mark Dodici,Scott Tremaine,Yanqin Wu*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 星系中心恒星双星的性质对多种观测现象很重要。研究更新了先前的模型，发现双星轨道收缩后可能形成紧密双星而非碰撞，且新考虑的因素显著增加了此类事件的发生率。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 星系中心的恒星双星与高速恒星、X射线双星等观测现象相关，但其性质尚不明确。过去研究认为部分双星会收缩或碰撞，但模型需要更新。

Method: 重新审视了恒星潮汐和黑洞潮汐场导致的轨道收缩模型，加入了向量共振松弛和恒星扰动的效应，并通过数值实验验证。

Result: 数值实验显示，在离Sgr A* 1 pc处，1/5的双星会在主序阶段发生轨道收缩；在0.01 pc内，比例升至3/5。

Conclusion: 研究发现双星更可能形成紧密双星而非碰撞，且新效应显著提高了此类事件的发生率，对银河系中心年轻双星的观测有重要意义。

Abstract: Stellar binaries in galactic centers are relevant to several observable
phenomena, including hypervelocity stars, X-ray binaries, and mergers of stars
and compact objects; however, we know little about the properties of these
binaries. Past works have suggested that a small fraction of them should
contract to a few stellar radii or collide, due to the co-operation of stellar
tides and the eccentricity oscillations induced by the strong tidal field of
the central massive black hole. We revisit this model with several updates. We
first argue that when a binary's pericenter separation is driven down to a few
stellar radii, diffusive excitation of stellar tides should quickly contract
the orbit, saving the stars from collision. Instead, the stars should end up as
a very tight binary. We then show that vector resonant relaxation and
perturbations from passing stars -- effects not included in past models --
dramatically increase the prevalence of such encounters. In numerical
experiments, we find that 1 in 5 binaries around 1 pc from Sgr A* should
tidally contract in this way while still on the main sequence. This rate climbs
to 3 in 5 around 0.01 pc, inward of which it plateaus. We briefly discuss
observable implications of these results, with particular attention to young
stellar binaries in the Galactic Center.

### [2] [The Challenge in Illuminating the Invisible: Constraining LyC Escape with Bayesian Modelling and Symbolic Regression](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.02908)
*Amanda Stoffers,Sandro Tacchella,Charlotte Simmonds,Benjamin D. Johnson,Roberto Maiolino*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: 该论文通过贝叶斯SED拟合方法，间接估计了LyC逃逸分数（f_esc^LyC），并在低红移LyC调查（LzLCS）样本上验证了方法的有效性，发现了从极低泄漏到高泄漏的广泛结果，并提出了新的UV β斜率与f_esc^LyC关系。

  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 宇宙再电离时期（EoR）的星系LyC辐射直接观测被星际介质吸收所阻碍，需要间接方法来推断电离光子的逃逸分数。本研究旨在开发并验证此类方法，通过分析低红移类似星系的数据。

Method: 使用基于Prospector的非参数化恒星形成历史、灵活尘埃衰减曲线、自洽的气体发射和光纤孔径损失校正的贝叶斯SED拟合框架，联合拟合宽带测光和发射线通量，并在LzLCS样本上应用六种模型进行验证。

the Epoch of Reionization (EoR) are impeded by absorption in the intergalactic

methods on local analogues of the high-redshift galaxies with directly detected

Prospector, which incorporates a non-parametric star-formation history, a

### [3] [A dust condensation instability in AGN atmospheres: failed winds and the broad line region](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.02920)
*James E. Owen,Douglas N. C. Lin*

Main category: astro-ph.GA

TL;DR: AGN的宽线区形成机制尚不明确，研究发现吸积盘的垂直结构中尘埃凝结会导致大气层不稳定，形成尘埃喷泉，这与FRADO模型一致。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究AGN宽线区的物理成因，重点关注尘埃凝结对吸积盘大气层垂直结构的影响。

Method: 通过吸积盘垂直结构模型分析尘埃凝结和中央辐射的影响，并进行局部2D流体动力学模拟验证线性不稳定性。

Result: 大气层由于尘埃凝结变得不稳定，形成尘埃喷泉，模拟显示其速度分散超过1000 km/s。

Conclusion: 尘埃凝结导致的线性不稳定性和非线性尘埃喷泉现象支持FRADO模型作为AGN宽线区的解释。

Abstract: Active galactic nuclei (AGN) are important drivers of galactic evolution;
however, the underlying physical processes governing their properties remain
uncertain. In particular, the specific cause for the generation of the
broad-line region is unclear. There is a region where the underlying accretion
disc atmosphere becomes cool enough for dust condensation. Using models of the
disc's vertical structure, accounting for dust condensation and irradiation
from the central source, we show that their upper atmospheres become extended,
dusty, and radiation-pressure-supported. Due to the density--temperature
dependence of dust condensation, this extended atmosphere forms as the dust
abundance slowly increases with height, resulting in density and temperature
scale heights considerably larger than the gas pressure scale height. We show
that such an atmospheric structure is linearly unstable. An increase in the gas
density raises the dust sublimation temperature, leading to an increased dust
abundance, a higher opacity, and hence a net vertical acceleration. Using
localised 2D hydrodynamic simulations, we demonstrate the existence of our
linear instability. In the non-linear state, the disc atmosphere evolves into
``fountains'' of dusty material that are vertically launched by radiation
pressure before being exposed to radiation from the central source, which
sublimates the dust and shuts off the radiative acceleration. These dust-free
clumps then evolve ballistically, continuing upward before falling back towards
the disc under gravity. This clumpy ionized region has velocity dispersions
$\gtrsim 1000$ km/s. This instability and our simulations are representative of
the Failed Radiatively Accelerated Dusty Outflow (FRADO) model proposed for the
AGN broad-line region.

</details>
